Brief Bio

I am a PHD student in Cener for Turbulence Control(ctc.hitsz.edu.cn), Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen), under the supervision of Prof. Bernd Noack(berndnoack.com).

My research focuses on developing highly efficient optimization methods and advancing adaptative and robust machine learning for turbulence control.

The practical engineering plants are usually unknown and complex, and may include unfriendly features to some optimizers. In machine learning and optimization, the algorithms with adaptive variants can be more competitive in face of different plants. It is expensive to recollect the needed training data and rebuild the models with varying operating conditions of turbulence control in the real world, which is what traditional machine learning does. Modern machine learning integrating robustness and adaptivity would be necessary to reduce the need and effort to recollect the training data.
